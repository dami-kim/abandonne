My family    26 Mar 2018   Paris



I have waited for the vacation with my family this summer. I imagined myself going to a nice restaurant where we could see the Mediterranean in Croatia. However, yesterday, my mother told me that my father would not come to Paris because it was difficult for him to take one or two weeks off. I was sad.

In fact, my father has never been in Europe in his life. My mother, my brother and I, we have been there several times. I think that it is very difficult for him because he is used to his work on specific schedule. Nevertheless, I wish that he spends a time in a foreign country and change his thoughts...
