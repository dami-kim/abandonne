I went to this place because I liked Willy Ronis’ works.
Willy Ronis (France, 1910–2009)
La passerelle, Champigny-sur-Marne, 1957 He is famous French photographer. Here is the background of one photo of his. La Marne, born to la Seine, runs towards the southeast.
I left Paris with my camera. It was my first time to come near la Marne. I walked down every alley. There were the tree leaves, forest and silent river. They had the same sentiment as in the Ronis’ photo. Yet, instead of the vibrant children, the peaceful old men fished.
The river reflects us as always.

6 Nov 2018
