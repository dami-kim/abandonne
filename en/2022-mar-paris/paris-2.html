22 Sep 2018 - Opéra

Some black men wait for tourists at the entrance of the park Montmartre. They put thread bracelets on tourists’ wrists without them noticing. After, they say “Because you buy it, give me money.” If the tourists refuse, their friends appear and surround the tourists suddenly. This is why there are so many black men in the park in front of the cathedral Sacré-Cœur.
I thought that these bracelets are what makes Montmartre perfect.
